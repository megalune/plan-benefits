Your Hally® Experience

Whether you’re looking for extra tools to help you be healthy or want to manage your plan at your convenience, Hally health and the secure member website are here for you.

Exploring Hally Health

Hally health is all about helping you live your healthiest life, giving you access to plenty of resources to help you stay healthy with no login required. You’ll find all this and more:

  • Exercise classes, health courses and cooking demos.
  • The Hally blog.
  • Hally Healthcast, a monthly podcast led by medical experts.
  • Health tips, coaching and information on care coordination.
  • Tools and resources for asthma, diabetes and high blood pressure.

Explore hally.com

Managing Your Plan

Get secure, instant access to your coverage anytime by logging in to the Hally® app or hally.com. Manage your health plan and get the care you need anytime, anywhere with:

  • All your account activities in one place.
  • Virtual ID card access.
  • Ability to search providers, pharmacies, covered drugs and more.
  • Quick access to virtual visits.
  • Doctor search and cost estimates.
